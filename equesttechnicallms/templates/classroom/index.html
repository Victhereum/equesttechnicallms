Our vision

Our vision at Equesttech is a learning platform that aims to provide the best learning environment for students by recommending qualified tutors to make sure students get the best of their payment. It also provide a safe learning environment to our Tutors and ensures that everyone is treated with respect.

We know that most times, students struggle to assimilate a lot of information given to them during lectures or classes and usually this is not enough to impart the necessary knowledge. Thus we created an easy-to-use platform that will enable students to relate to tutors and get the most out of learning.

We also give our tutors the best opportunity to share their knowledge gained through experience and earn an income out of it.
